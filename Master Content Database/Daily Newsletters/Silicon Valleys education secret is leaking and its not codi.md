## DECISION FATIGUE (n.)

The mental exhaustion resulting from making too many choices, depleting your willpower and leading to poor decisions or decision avoidance. First identified by social psychologist Dr. Roy Baumeister, it explains why choosing between 347 homeschool curricula makes even the most dedicated parents consider the merits of unschooling.
