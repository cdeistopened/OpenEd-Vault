## Sedation Nation

Katherine Boyle calls it ["one of the greatest evils"](https://content.opened.co/e3t/Ctc/JA+113/d5pV-k04/VW2pr25xwRg0W9bx4-b5FrHDfW2VV8NG5zBGrNN5XFYng3qgz0W8wLKSR6lZ3nzW8P9J4g374SfzW7nGyXJ7g6RqkW6tD1SK2tkj0HW4kvyw08XPqchW4DZ7Sl99pZFcN1rlW2Lj78h5W56FQxt1Tg55nVB8sx57DmF8TW8YKh7H8pQwL2N6mZXn26SPKBW2-zGtN3xHnn-W7Y25Qc8sx1PtW8ZmHxp2DsDSjN1dkZ0nNQ_PNW7yKL5c6YcGr5W51JJZ746FLbjF7dSVLRQBWvW4T9hRs7Qh1GNW4YbMcw6kCcCFVvTSdf4Tk5ztW1r6SfV3mZ97kW11gWG56HnqNxVKSwfg8XxXKDW1j2_gT12TR4wW5TYvmc1X4MzjVjsPTH3l3K38N8H3zhZr9x87W31zhnn46qgKWdS36Y404) of her 90s childhood: "the sedation and casual drugging of young boys for acting like boys."
