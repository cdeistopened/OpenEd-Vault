## **MAGIC WORDS THAT STOP HELICOPTER PARENTING**

"*"It's a learning opportunity.‚Äù*

According to [**a new Yale study**](https://letgrow.org/yale-helicopter-parenting/?utm_medium=email&_hsmi=2&utm_content=2&utm_source=hs_email) these four simple words might be the cure for helicopter parenting. Researchers asked kids to put on hockey gear while giving parents different instructions:

- Half were told this was a chance for their child to learn a life skill
- Half were simply told it was a way to engage with the museum

Parents who saw it as a "learning opportunity" intervened 50% less often (4.4 times vs. 8.6 times for the control group).

Same task, different framing, dramatically different parental behavior.
