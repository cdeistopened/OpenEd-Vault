## What Was Added

### 1_Social_Media_Principles.md - NEW SECTIONS:

#### Testing & Iteration (Expanded)
- **CTR Specific Benchmarks**: < 4% (needs revision), 4-6% (acceptable), 6%+ (strong), 7-8% (excellent)
- **Revision Strategy**: Can revise title/thumbnail months later to restart algorithmic distribution
- **Volume-Based Title Generation**: Write 15 different titles per video to avoid first-match bias

#### Thumbnail Psychology Deep Dive (NEW)
- **Shock Value Over Recognition**: Only use headshot if "George Clooney-famous"
- **Stock Photos > Hand-Drawn**: Illustrations don't work, use stock photos
- **AI-Generated Thumbnails**: Dall-E for Pixar-ish photorealism
- **Technical Rules**: Left-align text (avoid right-side UI overlap)
- **"Dial to 11" Strategy**: Push drama to absurd, then pull back one notch

#### The 15-Minute Prototyping Rule (NEW)
- **Core Philosophy**: If working longer than 15 minutes, that's a warning sign
- **90% Rule**: Last 10% takes 2.5x the time - diminishing returns
- **Blank Page Paralysis**: "Almost everybody is a better editor than blank page creator"
- **First Draft Philosophy**: Complete it, don't evaluate while creating
- **Mindless Execution**: Jump in, complete, then evaluate

#### Platform-Specific Advanced Tactics (NEW)
- **Facebook Reels Monetization**: Meta encouraging cross-posting
- **Instagram Edits Restyle**: 6-second limit, multiple characters
- **Remix Feature**: Piggyback on trending content
- **Screen Recording from Phone**: Highest resolution method

#### Hook Categories (Expanded)
- **The "One Thing" Constraint Hook**: Condenses complex topics
- **Repeatable Gimmick Strategy**: Find what works, use repeatedly
- **Curiosity Gap Without Clickbait**: Deliver on promise in first 3 seconds
