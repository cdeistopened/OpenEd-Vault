# "5 Tools/Curricula for X" Format

**Production Time**: 10-15 minutes per video
**Format**: Easel or tablet with numbered list, you narrate
**Strategic Value**: Educational + high save rate + establishes expertise

---

## WHY THIS FORMAT WORKS

### Algorithmic Benefits:
- ✅ **Listicles perform**: Clear structure = higher completion rate
- ✅ **High save rate**: People bookmark for later = strong signal
- ✅ **Searchable**: "Best math curriculum" gets ongoing traffic
- ✅ **Educational**: YouTube/IG favor helpful content

### Brand Benefits:
- ✅ **Establishes expertise**: You know the landscape
- ✅ **OpenEd value prop**: Helps parents navigate options
- ✅ **Lead generation**: Links to OpenEd resources
- ✅ **Evergreen**: These don't age like news-based content

---

## THE MECHANICS

### Visual Setup Option A: Easel
1. Write "5 [Tools] for [Goal]" at top
2. Write numbers 1-5 down left side
3. Fill in as you talk
4. Or pre-write and reveal with page flips

### Visual Setup Option B: Tablet
1. Pre-design numbered list in Canva
2. Show on tablet as you talk
3. Can add visuals/logos

### Audio:
- Keep it conversational
- 15-20 seconds per tool
- Total: 90-120 seconds

## PRODUCTION TIPS

### Easel Method:
1. Pre-write all 5 on paper
2. Write #1 on easel while talking
3. Continue through #5
4. Total: 10-12 minutes filming

### Tablet Method:
1. Create numbered list in Canva (3 min)
2. Hold tablet, narrate through list
3. Total: 8 minutes filming

### Editing:
- Trim to 90-120 seconds
- Add text overlay of numbers/names
- Background music (optional)
- Total: 5 minutes editing

**Full Production: 15-20 minutes per video**
