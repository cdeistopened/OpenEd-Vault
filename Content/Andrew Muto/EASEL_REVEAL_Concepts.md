## FORMAT MECHANICS

### Setup:
1. Easel positioned with picturesque homestead background
2. You stand with back to camera, writing with big permanent marker
3. Sound of marker writing (ASMR element)
4. Turn around to reveal message
5. Hold for 5-8 seconds (allows replays)

### Why This Works (Andrew Muto Principles):
- ‚úÖ **Topic signal** through sound and motion
- ‚úÖ **Hook quickly** - reveal creates curiosity gap
- ‚úÖ **5-8 seconds** = multiple replays = algorithm boost
- ‚úÖ **McDonald's Test** - text must be instantly readable
- ‚úÖ **Authentic setting** - homestead = real life proof

## PRODUCTION SYSTEM

### Morning Batch (Film 10 in 60 minutes)

**Setup** (5 min):
- Position easel with homestead background
- Test lighting
- Have 10 messages pre-written on paper
- Stack of 10 clean easel pages ready

**Filming** (40 min):
- 4 minutes per video
- Write message (back to camera) - 90 sec
- Turn, reveal, hold 5-8 sec - 30 sec
- Rip off page, next message - 30 sec
- NO RETAKES unless major error

**Editing** (15 min):
- Trim to 8-15 seconds each
- Add subtle sound effect on reveal (optional)
- Export all 10
- 90 seconds per video editing

**Total**: 60 minutes = 10 videos
