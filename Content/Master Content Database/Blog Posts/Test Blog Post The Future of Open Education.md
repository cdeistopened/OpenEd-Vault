## Introduction

This is a test blog post to demonstrate the CMS functionality of our Webflow site. Open education represents a fundamental shift in how we think about learning, moving away from one-size-fits-all approaches toward personalized, flexible pathways that honor each child's unique needs and interests.

## The Open Education Philosophy

At its core, open education is about choice, flexibility, and customization. It recognizes that learning happens everywhere—not just in traditional classrooms—and empowers families to design educational experiences that truly fit their children.

## Key Principles

Open education builds on several foundational principles: student agency, family empowerment, resource diversity, and community connection. These principles guide families as they navigate the many options available to them.

## Conclusion
