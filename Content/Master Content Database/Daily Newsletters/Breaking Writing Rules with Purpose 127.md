## **From Struggling Reader to Published Author**

Jennifer Jenkins loved stories long before she could read them easily. After discovering she had dyslexia in college, everything changed: she fell in love with reading, became an author, and now leads [**StoryCon**](https://storycon.org/?utm_source=opened-daily.beehiiv.com&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=breaking-writing-rules-with-purpose-127), one of the largest young writer events in the country.

1. Start with "what if?" (but don't stop there)
2. Build your world with endless "whys"
