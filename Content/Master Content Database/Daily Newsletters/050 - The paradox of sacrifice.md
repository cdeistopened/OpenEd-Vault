‚ùù

**If you don't sacrifice for what you want, what you want becomes a sacrifice.**

"Sacrifice" can either mean investing time, energy, and sometimes comfort in pursuit of your goals‚Ä¶ or giving up on the things you desire.

Your willingness to sacrifice in the first sense of the word determines how much or little you have to sacrifice in the second sense.

These programs often meet 1-2 days per week (some more, some less), offering balance to virtual curriculums and online school.

## **Spice Up Your Lessons with ‚Äú10 Minute Tricks‚Äù**

Do you ever feel like your lessons are falling flat? Research shows that even the most attentive students struggle to focus after just 10 minutes of lecture. Earlier this week we[ shared Pam Barnhill‚Äôs ‚Äú10-minute rule](https://opened-daily.beehiiv.com/p/047-beyond-mere-choice?utm_source=opened-daily.beehiiv.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=047-beyond-mere-choice&_bhlid=8c9196e55b88e28711fc1e3be99c497aa2f80bd0&last_resource_guid=Post%3Aa34345fa-4408-4a92-9b67-d50c4871f16e)‚Äù for short learning sprints. Today, we‚Äôre offering three simple tricks to reset between these micro-lessons:

1. **Movement Snacks:** Quick stretches or jumping jacks boost concentration. University students who took 5-10 minute exercise breaks during long lectures felt more confident and less worried about making mistakes.
2. **Drawing to Learn:** When a topic lends itself to visuals, let your kids sketch it out. Simple drawings (even stick figures) can nearly double factual recall. For abstract ideas, try concept mapping.
3. **Rapid Reviews**: Quick recaps clarify misunderstandings. Use tools like [Kahoot](https://kahoot.com/?utm_source=opened-daily.beehiiv.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=050-the-paradox-of-sacrifice&_bhlid=66a394daeb3a679e4866741ac6cd53205d41223b) or [Quizizz](https://quizizz.com/?utm_source=opened-daily.beehiiv.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=050-the-paradox-of-sacrifice&_bhlid=74d95c37820485929965ebac84630ac5ae7b66b1) to ask a short review question and turn rapid reviews into fun, low-stakes quizzes.
