In our research for [***Open Education***](https://geni.us/opened?utm_source=opened-daily.beehiiv.com&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=it-s-here), we discovered that the most successful families aren't choosing between "school" or "not school"‚Äîthey're creating a third path that takes the best of multiple approaches.
