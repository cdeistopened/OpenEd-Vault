### The 5 Biggest Homeschooling Fears (Tl;dr)

**"What if I'm not good at it?"** → Plan on being bad at first. Give yourself permission to be a beginner.

**"What if I fail my kids?"** → You're probably doing better than you think. Focus on the journey, not just outcomes.

**"What if my kids fight all the time?"** → Fighting is a skill they're learning. Work on connection first.

**"I don't think I'm smart enough."** → You don't have to be. We have Google, AI, and endless curriculum options that don't require you to "teach" in a traditional sense. Learn alongside your kids.

**"How do I know what curriculum to use?"** → Start with your family values, not the "perfect" curriculum.

**The fix:** Name your fears, identify your values, and remember—you don't have to be perfect. You just have to keep trying.

### The Résumé is Dead

In 2025, AI writes job applications *and* screens them. The result? Hiring managers trust neither. [Michael Horn explains](https://content.opened.co/e3t/Ctc/JA+113/d5pV-k04/VVylQd2zZH3VW7_d1NF5_CXqMW15bp5B5Bf_6zN5n2LRP3qgz0W95jsWP6lZ3n3W6WGsnm6c7kCHW1-7_Hg8WnHrYW3B79Bl1xs2r-W5ny8Px8qdGQ6W2kjvsD6TzlVFN6_7lQFGBF3QW97gtML7fYcVbW9g9x2j4th1lkW2V25Bz44j5-CW95Mm9b8Zwj00W2R9jPc3V_HdzW1Q3vR-1r0WfyW3HdhnQ9b9tDzW1B34b-5x8kbdV_fKhw1FjKjqW78dzP52X265CW4DvbHh1HlMHLN9dMzBZQTpsHW2SlKQn1cSJT3V8qktS8vgwSjW5c-61v3nlr4VW69gsNp8cTmWZW5q6mtk5zcgQfW85SWYh3bD9cnW6p9lQ045-bR9W7zjgpR38R46TW32NmYR4rRNTFW88-45v1WTJZTVbhqhP5zcD7_W540XLJ4JDyxmf5LqrM-04): they call someone they trust and ask, "Know anyone good?"

In short, stop polishing bullet points. Optimize for referrals and evidence. Ship projects, collect references, and ask for warm intros. And if someone *does* ask for a traditional résumé? Well, there's always ChatGPT.
