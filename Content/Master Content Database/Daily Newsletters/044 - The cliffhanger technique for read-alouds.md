## The Cliffhanger Technique: A Secret Weapon for Read-Alouds

Want to supercharge your family read-aloud sessions? Try [**this counterintuitive tip**](https://simplycharlottemason.com/blog/reading-aloud-so-children-love-to-listen/?utm_source=opened-daily.beehiiv.com&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=044-the-cliffhanger-technique-for-read-alouds): **Stop at the good parts.**

**Here's why it works:**

1. **Enhances engagement:** Builds anticipation and encourages critical thinking as kids ponder, "What happens next?"
2. **Boosts creativity and retention:** Imaginations run wild in the interim, helping the story stick
3. **Teaches patience:** A valuable life skill in our instant-gratification world

**How to do it:**

- Identify climactic moments or unresolved situations
- Stop reading, even mid-chapter
- Resist the "one more page" pleas (stay strong, parents!)
- Encourage discussion, but don't assign it

Remember: This isn't about torturing your kids. It's about giving space for the story to marinate in their minds.
