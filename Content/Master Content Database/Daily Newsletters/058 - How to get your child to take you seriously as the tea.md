1. **Consistency:** ‚ÄúMy kids didn't take me seriously until I made us stick to a strict schedule." Try setting a routine: breakfast at 8:00, school starts at 9:00, with a predictable order of subjects.
2. **Engagement:** "I do my best to keep the quiet listening parts of lessons to no more than 10 to 15 minutes." Mix it up with movement and discussion to keep kids focused.
