Ever feel like you’re running a corporation instead of a home?

Clipboards. Timers. Stress.

That’s the _Homeschool CEO_ mindset—and it’s exhausting.

Here’s the truth: your kids don’t need a CEO.

They need a gardener.

A gardener sets the right conditions, gives care, and trusts the process.

Relax. The kids are going to be okay.

Fire the CEO. Be the gardener.
