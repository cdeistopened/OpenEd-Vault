Text: Sal Khan discusses the transformative potential of AI in education, proposing the idea of personalized AI tutors for every student and AI teaching assistants for educators. He emphasizes that AI can enhance writing skills by collaborating with students rather than replacing them, ultimately aiming to improve educational outcomes for all learners.
---
# CLIP 1: "How AI Could Transform Education"

## HOOK:

"I think we're at the cusp of using AI for probably the biggest positive transformation that education has ever seen." (0:33-0:42)

## SUPPORTING CONTENT:

- "And the way we're going to do that is by giving every student on the planet an artificially intelligent but amazing personal tutor." (0:44-0:51)
- "And we're going to give every teacher on the planet an amazing, artificially intelligent teaching assistant." (0:51-0:57)
- "That could take your average student and turn them into an exceptional student. It can take your below-average student and turn them into an above-average student." (1:41-1:50)
- "The student is against canceling student debt, and we've gotten very clear feedback... The students, the high school students especially, they're saying 'This is amazing to be able to fine-tune my arguments without fearing judgment.'" (7:16-7:32)

## ON-SCREEN CAPTION:

"What if AI could turn every student into an exceptional one?"

"The secret to turning EVERY student into a genius..."

# CLIP 2: "AI Doesn't Replace Writing, It Enhances It"

## HOOK:

"A lot of the narrative has been, 'It's going to do the writing for kids. Kids are not going to learn to write.'" (7:48-7:55)

## SUPPORTING CONTENT:

- "But we are showing that there's ways that the AI doesn't write for you, it writes with you." (7:55-8:00)
- "This is an activity where the student will write two sentences, and then the AI will write two sentences. And so they collaborate together on a story." (8:16-8:24)
- "The student writes, 'Beatrice was a misunderstood ghost. She wanted to make friends but kept scaring them by accident.'" (8:24-8:29)
- "And the AI says, 'Poor Beatrice, a lonely spirit yearning for companionship. One day she stumbled upon an old abandoned mansion,' etc." (8:29-8:36)
- "We think this is going to dramatically accelerate writing, not hurt it." (10:17-10:20)

## ON-SCREEN CAPTION:

"The writing tool that makes kids WANT to write..."
