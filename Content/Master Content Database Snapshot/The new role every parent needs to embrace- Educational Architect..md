The new role every parent needs to embrace: Educational Architect.  
  
Not teaching every subject.  
Not enforcing a rigid curriculum.  
Not accepting one-size-fits-all solutions.  
  
Instead, thoughtfully designing environments where learning happens naturally through:  
• Mixed learning formats  
• Real-world projects  
• Self-directed exploration  
• Evolving approaches  
  
Children don't need to be forced to learn. They need to be free to learn.
