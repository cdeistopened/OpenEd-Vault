Did you know our school system is based on a 200-year-old Prussian model designed to create obedient soldiers? Or that we grade students in ways that would make even elite athletes like LeBron James look like failures?

Isaac Morehouse and Matt Bowman expose the fundamental flaws in our education system in their category-defining new book "Open Education: How to Reimagine Learning, Ignite Curiosity, and Prepare Your Kids for Success."

In this episode, the authors break down Part One of their book, examining the historical traps of education, why standardization doesn't work, and their revolutionary "mastered or not yet" approach.
