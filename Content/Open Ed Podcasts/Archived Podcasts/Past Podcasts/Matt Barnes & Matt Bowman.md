Title:  
Why 'Weird' Wins: Tips for Young Entrepreneurs | Matt Barnes and Matt Bowman

  
Description:  
When normal is broken, weird wins. On the episode of The OpenEd Podcast, join Matt Bowman and Matt Barnes as they discuss why entrepreneurship might be the most powerful education tool we've never fully utilized.

Former school board member turned "Parent Whisperer" reveals why traditional education is failing our kids - and how entrepreneurship might be the solution. Matt Barnes shares powerful insights from his experience on 10 school boards and raising three children who each took radically different educational paths.  
Learn why some of the most successful young people are breaking away from traditional education, and how parents can support their children's unique learning journeys without fear.
