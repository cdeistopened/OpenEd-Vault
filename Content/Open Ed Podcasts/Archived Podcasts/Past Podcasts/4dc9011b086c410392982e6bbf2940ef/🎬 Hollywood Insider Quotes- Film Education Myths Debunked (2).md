## Slide 1:

"Film reaches every type and allows people of every walk of life to have an important part in the filmmaking process."  
—Chet Thomas, former Dreamworks executive

## Slide 2:

### MYTH: Film sets must be high-stress environments

"Life is just too short to create more stress than you need when the stress is already there... it's great to have fun while getting it done and lifting others."  
—Chet Thomas

## Slide 3:

### MYTH: You need film school to break into Hollywood

"We've seen students who have proven themselves... we're looking at possibly bringing some of these students in. What Sneak On is built to do is filter out and find the best of the best."  
—Darrin Fletcher, Hollywood veteran

## Slide 4:

"We can employ every single classroom of a high school into the process of making a film... marketing, business, advertising, acting, music, sciences... I can employ every class into filmmaking."  
—Darrin Fletcher

## Slide 5:

### MYTH: You need to be creative to work in film

"It takes all types. It takes the creative kind... the analytical... the person that's great at math... the person who's super social. There's a place for that person."  
—Chet Thomas

## Slide 6:

"Tenacity is the one aspect in this industry... you don't have it, you ain't gonna make it. The harder you work, the more likely it is to happen."  
—Darrin Fletcher

_Quotes from Chet Thomas and Darrin Fletcher of Sneak On The Lot_
