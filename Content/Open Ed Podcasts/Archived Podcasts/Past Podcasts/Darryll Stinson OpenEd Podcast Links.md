        Come in there and SET IT OFF from the jump. Set the tone. When you do that? They start raising their temperature to match YOURS.  
          
        I gave this advice recently on the @openedhq podcast. They're building a movement of engaged parents transforming their kids education.

        Don't reflect the energy in the room. CREATE the energy you want.
