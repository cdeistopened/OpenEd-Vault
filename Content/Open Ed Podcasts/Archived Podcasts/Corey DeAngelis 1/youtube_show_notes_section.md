## YouTube Show Notes

### Episode Description

Fund families, not systems - Corey DeAngelis explains how this one simple change could transform American education and why the school choice movement is winning faster than ever before.

- How the pandemic accidentally created an education revolution
- Why 17 states now have universal school choice (up from just 1 five years ago)
- The "unbundling" of education and what it means for your child's future
- Why parents don't need "experts" to make good educational choices
