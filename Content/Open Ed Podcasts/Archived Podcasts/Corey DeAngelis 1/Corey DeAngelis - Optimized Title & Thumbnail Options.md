# Corey DeAngelis - Optimized Title & Thumbnail Options
*Applying the YouTube Title & Thumbnail Framework*

## **Core Strategy: Parent Revolution Becoming Mainstream**

**Focus:** Position school choice as the natural evolution of education, not an attack on public schools
**Tone:** Empowering, forward-looking, inclusive
**Target Emotion:** "This is the future, and parents are leading the way"

---

## **TOP 3 RECOMMENDED OPTIONS**

### **Option 1: The "Please Watch This" Formula** ⭐ **RECOMMENDED**

**Title:** "If you care about your kid's education, please watch this"
**Thumbnail Text:** "PARENTS ARE WINNING"
**Thumbnail Design:** Corey speaking passionately + diverse group of parents with children
**Synergy Type:** Direct Appeal + Victory Narrative

**Why This Works:**
- Creates immediate identification with parent viewers
- "Parents are winning" positions this as a positive movement, not an attack
- Appeals to the core OpenEd audience (parents who care about education)
- Uses Andrew's proven "please watch this" formula

### **Option 2: The Curiosity Gap Hook**

**Title:** "17 states just did something that will change education forever"
**Thumbnail Text:** "THE PARENT REVOLUTION IS HERE"
**Thumbnail Design:** Map of US with 17 states highlighted in bright colors
**Synergy Type:** Curiosity Gap + Data Shock

**Why This Works:**
- Specific number creates credibility and urgency
- "Will change education forever" creates massive curiosity gap
- Map visual shows real-world momentum
- Positions change as inevitable, not controversial

### **Option 3: The Transformation Story**

**Title:** "How parents quietly revolutionized education in 3 years"
**Thumbnail Text:** "FROM 1 STATE TO 17"
**Thumbnail Design:** Before/after split showing transformation
**Synergy Type:** Before → After + Momentum

**Why This Works:**
- "Quietly" suggests organic, grassroots movement
- "3 years" shows rapid, recent change
- Focuses on parent agency, not political conflict
- Shows concrete progress with state numbers

- **Corey's face:** Speaking passionately, confident expression
- **Supporting visuals:** Maps, families, educational symbols

- **Typography:** Bold, high-contrast text

### **Text Overlay Options:**
- "PARENTS ARE WINNING"
- "17 STATES AND COUNTING..."
- "THE REVOLUTION IS HERE"
- "YOUR KID, YOUR CHOICE"
- "FROM 1 STATE TO 17"

- **Size:** 1280×720 (16:9 ratio)
- **File size:** ≤ 2 MB
- **Safe area:** Keep key text inside 91% of frame
- **Mobile readability:** Test at 120×68 px scale

## **KEY MESSAGING PRINCIPLES**

### **What to Emphasize:**
- ✅ Parent empowerment and agency
- ✅ Real-world success and momentum
- ✅ Natural evolution, not radical change
- ✅ Inclusivity and accessibility
- ✅ Evidence-based progress

### **What to Avoid:**
- ❌ Anti-public school rhetoric
- ❌ Political polarization
- ❌ "Us vs them" framing
- ❌ Doomsday predictions
- ❌ Complex policy explanations

### **Tone Guidelines:**
- **Empowering:** Parents are taking control
- **Optimistic:** This is working and growing
- **Inclusive:** Everyone benefits from choice
- **Evidence-based:** Show real progress
- **Forward-looking:** This is the future

---

*The goal is to position school choice as the natural evolution of education—something that's happening because it works, not because traditional education is failing. Parents are leading this change because they know what's best for their children.*
