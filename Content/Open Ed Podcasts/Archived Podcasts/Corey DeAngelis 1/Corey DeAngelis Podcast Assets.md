## Cold Open

### Cold Open Script (25-35 seconds)

**Format:** Dialogue Exchange Format (Question → Setup → Revelation → Cliffhanger)

```
[COLD OPEN]

ISAAC: "If you had to identify just ONE fundamental change that would transform American education, what would it be?"

COREY: "The one simple step is to fund families directly instead of systems."

ISAAC: "That's it? Just change where the money goes?"

COREY: "When you think about it, that's the root of all the problems. The government has a monopoly on $800 billion of K-12 education funding each year. And when you fund the institutions directly..."

[CUT before resolution]
```

### Why This Cold Open Works:
1. **Contradiction Hook:** Challenges conventional wisdom that education reform is complex
2. **Emotional Escalator:** Builds from simple question to revelation of systemic problem
3. **Unfinished Symphony:** Cuts at maximum tension before revealing full explanation
4. **Curiosity Stack:** Creates multiple questions the listener must have answered:
   - How would this simple change fix everything?
   - What happens when you fund institutions directly?
   - What evidence supports this claim?

### Technical Elements:
- **Total length:** ~30 seconds
- **Individual clips:** Each 5-8 seconds (optimal for processing)
- **Natural conversation rhythm:** Preserves back-and-forth dynamic
- **Hard cuts:** No transitions or effects needed
- **Ends on incomplete statement:** Creates maximum curiosity gap

This cold open directly supports the recommended title "How to Fix American Education in One Simple Step" and creates an irresistible knowledge gap that viewers must fill by watching the full episode.
