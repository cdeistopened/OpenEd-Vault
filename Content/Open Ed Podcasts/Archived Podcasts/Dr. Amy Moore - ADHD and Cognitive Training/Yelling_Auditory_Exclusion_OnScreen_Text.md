## Visual Design Recommendations

### Text Styling:
- **Font:** Bold, sans-serif (Arial, Helvetica, or similar)

- **Animation:** Fade in/out, or slide up (not distracting)

### Background:
- **Option A:** Blurred video background (speaker visible but text readable)
