## THE WINNING QUOTE

### Selected Quote (Timestamp: 12:32.406)

**Full Quote:**
> "You're yelling at them and it looks like they're ignoring you. Honestly, they're struggling to hear the words that you're saying when you're yelling, because they're in fight or flight."

**Extended Version (for longer clip):**
> "Something very interesting happens when we yell at our kids. That alarm goes off, they go into fight, flight or freeze. The ears can't process language in fight or flight. It's called auditory exclusion. And so again, intelligently designed to only be able to hear low frequency sounds like that tiger rustling in the bush behind us, not mom's voice yelling at us. So then it looks like your child is ignoring you, right? You're yelling at them and it looks like they're ignoring you. Honestly, they're struggling to hear the words that you're saying when you're yelling, because they're in fight or flight."
