### Recommended Cold Open (Highest Drama Potential)

**Option 1: The Transformation Contradiction**
```
[COLD OPEN - MONTAGE]
[44:14] "I've been interested in and around education for my whole career and have never really felt like the whole thing is going to be changed."
[32:48] "We know what we need to do in public education in this country and we're just refusing to do it."
[12:25] "We took a group of kids who were performing on average at the 13th percentile of nationally recognized standardized tests."
[12:43] "And by the end of that second year with us, the majority were on grade level."
[15:16] "And that really bugged the crap out of me. And I wanted to do something that was going to fix that."
```

**Why this works**:
- Creates immediate contradiction (can't be changed vs. we know how to fix it)
- Shows dramatic transformation (13th percentile to grade level)
- Emotional peak ("bugged the crap out of me")
- Ends on motivation/action
- Aligns with OpenEd audience frustration with system
