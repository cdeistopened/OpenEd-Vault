## Universal Production Specs

**Video Format:**
- 9:16 aspect ratio (vertical)
- 1080x1920 resolution
- 30fps minimum
- Clear audio mixing (dialogue prioritized)

**Text Overlays:**
- Large, bold sans-serif font
- High contrast (white text on dark background or vice versa)
- Appear within first 2 seconds
- Readable at thumbnail size

**Branding:**
- OpenEd logo in corner (subtle)
- Consistent color scheme
- Episode number/guest name in description

**Accessibility:**
- Full captions on all clips
- Descriptive audio where appropriate
- Alt text for social media posts
