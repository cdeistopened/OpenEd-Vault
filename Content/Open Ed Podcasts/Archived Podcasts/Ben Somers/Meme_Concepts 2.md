## Implementation Notes

**For Newsletter**: 
- Pick ONE meme concept
- Place at end of newsletter before signature
- Format as image with caption underneath
- OR just describe the meme and say "Send this to a friend who needs to hear this"

**For Social Media**:
- Create actual meme images for Instagram/Facebook/LinkedIn
- Use Drake Pointing for Instagram (performs well)
- Use Two Buttons for Twitter/X (controversy drives engagement)
- Use Brain Expansion for LinkedIn (positive, professional)

**Design specs**:
- Square format (1080x1080) for Instagram
- Keep text large and readable
- Use Recess.gg or OpenEd branding subtly in corner
- Make sure it works without caption for shareability
