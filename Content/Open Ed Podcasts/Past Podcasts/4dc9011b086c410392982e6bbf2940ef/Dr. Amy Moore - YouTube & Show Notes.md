## YouTube Description

You think your child is ignoring you when you yell. But Dr. Amy Moore reveals the shocking neuroscience: **their brain literally can't process language during fight-or-flight.**

It's not defiance. It's auditory exclusion—an evolutionary survival mechanism.

**Dr. Amy Moore** is the host of [Brainy Moms Podcast](https://www.thebrainymoms.com/) and specializes in cognitive psychology and ADHD education. Her research spans 5,000+ ADHD brains.
