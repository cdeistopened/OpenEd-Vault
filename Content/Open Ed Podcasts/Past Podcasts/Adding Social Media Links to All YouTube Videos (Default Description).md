### Purpose

To ensure all upcoming YouTube videos automatically include social media links in their descriptions.

### Procedure

1. **Access YouTube Studio**
    - Go to YouTube Studio.
2. **Navigate to Settings**
    - Click on the **Settings** icon.
3. **Select Upload Defaults**
    - In the Settings menu, select **Upload defaults**.
4. **Edit Description**
    - In the Upload defaults section, locate the **Description** field.
    - Add your social media links in this Description field, formatted as:
        
        [https://x.com/OpenEdHQ](https://x.com/OpenEdHQ)  
        (use https:// and it will show up as an icon.
        
5. **Save Changes**
    - Scroll down and click **Save** to apply the changes.
6. **Verify Changes**
    - When you upload a new video, confirm that the social media links are automatically included in the description.

### Notes

- This process ensures that all future videos will have the specified social media links without needing to add them manually each time.
- You can edit or update the links in the Upload defaults at any time if your social media information changes.

### Example

1. **YouTube Studio**
    - Open YouTube Studio.
2. **Settings**
    - Go to **Settings**.
3. **Upload Defaults**
    - Select **Upload defaults**.
4. **Description Field**
    - Add social media links: e.g., `Follow us on Twitter: @YourTwitterHandle`.
5. **Save**
    - Click **Save**.
6. **Verification**
    - Upload a new video and check the description for the included links.

This SOP ensures consistency and saves time by automating the inclusion of social media links in YouTube video descriptions.
