## OpenEd's Unique Angle

### The Thesis

Charlotte Mason isn't a rigid curriculum - it's a philosophy that aligns with what modern neuroscience tells us about how children learn best. OpenEd families are already practicing CM principles without knowing it.

1. **Neuroscience Backing** (from Jean Lee podcast)

   - Validates CM's short lessons: "persistence of information" matters

3. **Real Parent Testimonials**
