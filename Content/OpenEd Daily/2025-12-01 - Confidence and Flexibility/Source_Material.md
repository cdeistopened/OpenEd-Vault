## TREND: Mom's Journey Through All Three School Types

**Source:** https://www.businessinsider.com/kids-attend-public-private-homeschool-pros-cons-2025-11

**Key Points:**
- Mom started as "crunchy mama" planning to homeschool, distrustful of public schools
- First child: Virtual charter school → private online school for gifted learners → grade-skipped
- Circumstances changed (became single mom of 3), tried traditional homeschool, then enrolled all in public school "with great reluctance"
- Result: Oldest and youngest THRIVED as extroverts in public school
- First grader wasn't thriving → pulled her for online charter
- Key insight: "An ideal education didn't just look different for my family, but for each child within it"
- Current state: Each child on different path (private academy, online with GIEP, homeschool)
- Quote: "When I nurture their unique educational needs, each kid feels like their talents are meaningful, so they become more engaged"
