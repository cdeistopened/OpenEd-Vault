## Concept 1: Policy Failure → Grassroots Success (Nevada Story)
**Core Insight:** Nevada's ESA program was blocked by courts in 2015 before a single family could use it. But that policy failure created space for something better—95,000 microschools serving 1.5 million students nationwide. Sometimes losing the policy fight means winning something bigger.

**Context:** Grant Hewitt's experience working on Nevada ESA, Don Soifer and Ashley's pivot to microschool support, North Las Vegas partnership delivering 125% academic growth at 1/4 to 1/3 the cost

**Emotion/Value:** Empowerment, resilience, "you don't need permission," optimism in the face of defeat

**Human Desires:** Freedom From Fear, Survival & Success, Safety of Tribe

**Potential Angles:** Before/After, Failure Story, Reverse Framing, Pattern Recognition
